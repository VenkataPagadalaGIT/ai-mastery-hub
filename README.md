## What Is This?

AI Mastery Hub is a **free, open-source learning system** that connects 233+ curated resources across a structured 18-week roadmap. It's built for anyone who wants to learn AI seriously—from Python fundamentals to building and deploying LLM applications.

Three interconnected resources:
1. **🗺️ AI Learning Roadmap** (23 topics, 6 phases, 18 weeks)
2. **🧠 AI Concepts Encyclopedia** (110 concepts, 10 categories)
3. **👥 Top 100 AI Contributors** (30+ fields each, fully searchable)

All connected. All free. All yours.

## How It All Connects

The true power of AI Mastery Hub is how everything connects:

```
🎯 Concept
   ↓ (What is Backpropagation?)
🗺️ Roadmap
   ↓ (Topic 11: Neural Networks)
👥 Expert
   ↓ (Yann LeCun - CNN pioneer)
📚 Sources
   ↓ (Papers, talks, courses, repos)
🏗️ Build
   ↓ (Clone repo, implement, customize)
⭐ Next Concept
```

**Example Journey:**
1. Look up **"Transformer"** in Encyclopedia
2. Find it in **Topic 15: Transformers & Attention** (Week 11)
3. Discover **Ashish Vaswani** (Transformer creator)
4. Watch his talks, read "Attention Is All You Need"
5. Clone HuggingFace transformers repo
6. Fine-tune a BERT model for your use case
7. Move to **Topic 20: Fine-Tuning & RAG**

**Nothing is isolated. Everything builds on everything else.**

## Getting Started

### 1. **Pick Your Path**
   - **Complete Journey:** Follow the 18-week Roadmap from Week 1
   - **Deep Dive:** Start with a specific phase (e.g., "I want to learn NLP")
   - **Concept-First:** Look up a concept in the Encyclopedia first

### 2. **Find Your Resources**
   - Each roadmap topic links to curated videos, courses, repos, and books
   - Each concept links to multiple learning materials
   - Follow the experts to discover their talks, papers, and projects

### 3. **Learn by Doing**
   - Don't just watch. **Build.**
   - 4 practice milestones force you to implement
   - Clone repos, modify them, build something original

### 4. **Stay Current**
   - Topic 28 teaches you to read research papers
   - Follow the 100 experts (Twitter, Substack, blogs)
   - Join communities (Papers with Code, Hugging Face, Discord)
